Wires

Wires allow energy to flow and are important to every circuit.

BreadBoard

BreadBoards are where we connect all of our other components. Allow energy to flow among rows.

Battery

Batteries store the energy we use to power our components. For energy to flow the electricty must have the ability to flow from positive to negative.

Battery Connector

Battery Connectors allow us to connect the Battery to the positve and negative columns of the Breadboard.

Resistor

Resistors are special wires that dampen the electrical system that flows through them. Help protect electrical components that cant handle too much energy.

LED

LEDs emit light when energy flows through them. They allow us to visually see the flow of energy. *need to be protected by the resistor
